About

Big Vee, an American, NY/UK Drill Rapper, songwriter, and producer, is making waves in the music scene. With an ethereal yet hard-hitting style, he effortlessly flows over New York drill beats, captivating listeners with hypnotic rhythms and slick notes.

Inspired by legends like Pop Smoke, Five Foreign, and Kay Flock, Big Vee's passion for New York and Brooklyn Drill Songs propelled him forward. He inked his first record deal at just 15, kickstarting his promising career.

Initially drawn to rap through Hardcore Hip-Hop icons like Eminem, Kendrick Lamar, J. Cole, and 2Pac, Big Vee expanded his horizons. Starting as an underground rapper, he gained recognition through mixtapes and impressive freestyles.

Transitioning from Trap beats to drilling beats, Big Vee's debut single, "Deja Vu," showcased his versatility as an artist. Co-producing tracks like "No Flex," "M.I.A," and "Deja Vu," he displayed his knack for crafting beats.

Utilizing platforms like Instagram, Big Vee showcased his skills, captivating fans with his freestyles and diss tracks. With "Deja Vu" selling 1000 copies by 2021 and notable appearances in high-school rap battles, he solidified his place in the rap game.

Big Vee's dedication, creativity, and passion shine through his music. With his distinct NY/UK Drill Rap sound, he's set to leave a lasting impact. Keep an eye on this rising star as he continues to captivate with his unique style and lyrical finesse.
